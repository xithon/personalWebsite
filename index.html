About me

Hi there, I'm Luke.

Currently, I am in my final year of an integrated Masters Engineering course at Durham University. I've specialised in Electrical Engineering after studying General Engineering for 2 years. I'm spending half my time working on a final year project using machine learning to classify defects in images of paint samples.

I also enjoy mixing live and recorded sound, and setting up lighting and AV equipment.
